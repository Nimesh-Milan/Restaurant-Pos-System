What's Included

Five powerful modules, one system

Every part of your restaurant operation is covered — from the moment a customer walks in to the moment they pay and leave.

01
🛒

Order Management

Staff select a table, browse the full menu, add items with quantities, and send the order to the kitchen with a single tap. Multiple tables can be open at the same time.

02

Billing & Receipts

Bills are automatically generated from the order — no manual adding. The bill shows all items, quantities, subtotal, tax, and total. Discounts can be applied. Receipts can be printed or saved as PDF.

Core Feature

Payment Tracking

Cashier records whether payment is by cash or card. For cash, the system automatically calculates change. Every payment is logged with a timestamp and linked to its order permanently.

How It Works

From customer arrival to payment done

The entire restaurant process is handled in 5 simple steps — all connected, all automatic.

🪑

Customer Arrives

Staff opens the system and selects the table number to start a new order

📋

Order is Taken

Items are picked from the digital menu with quantities and any special notes

🧾

Bill Generated

One click generates the full bill automatically with all items and totals

✅

Payment Done

Payment recorded, receipt printed, table cleared and ready for next customer

User Roles

The right access for every person

Two user types — each with their own login and access level so your staff only sees what they need.

👤

Admin

Owner / Manager
  • Full access to all modules
  • Manage menu items and prices
  • View and export all sales reports
  • Manage inventory and stock levels
  • Add or remove staff accounts
  • Apply discounts to any order
  • Change system settings
💁

Cashier

Counter Staff
  • Take and manage table orders
  • Generate bills for any table
  • Process cash and card payments
  • Print or save receipts
  • View open orders across tables
  • Cannot access reports or settings
